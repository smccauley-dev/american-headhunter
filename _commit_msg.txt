feat(billing): Phase 5.4 Slice 2 — promotion expiry (all three modes)

Daily ExpirePromotionClaims job: 30/7/1-day reminder emails (idempotent,
guarded by reminder_*_sent_at) + expiry processing that branches on each
promo's on_expiration mode.

- downgrade_free: claim expired, unlinked from subscription, revert to free.
- auto_charge: real Stripe paid subscription at the granted tier's live price
  (StripeService::createSubscription); claim -> converted. No payment method
  or unsynced price falls back to a free downgrade rather than leaving the
  member mid-conversion.
- pause_account: new users.status 'paused' (identity migration + CHECK);
  AuthService blocks paused login like suspended; never overrides a moderation
  state.

PromotionExpirationService::reactivate() lifts a pause when the member starts
paying again, wired into BillingService::subscribe and the
checkout.session.completed webhook path.

PromotionExpiringMail (TemplatedMailable) renders all three windows; copy
reflects the expiry mode. Scheduled daily at 06:00.

Tests: PromotionExpirationServiceTest (12 green) — all modes, auto-charge
fallback paths, reactivate, reminder dispatch + idempotency. Billing/webhook/
auth suites green (48).

Follow-up: paused members are bounced from /member + login, so a self-service
reactivation UX is still needed for pause_account to be end-to-end usable; the
billing-layer reactivation is in place and tested.
